Privacy

Short version: this site keeps nothing about you.

What this website collects

Nothing. This site sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and has no user accounts or databases. Anything you type into a booking or contact form on this site is used only to prepare a WhatsApp message on your own phone — it is never stored by, or transmitted to, this website.

WhatsApp & phone contact

When you tap a booking button, your details are handed to WhatsApp (or your phone app) and sent directly to Maya Nature Resort's reservation line, +256 773 883 760. From that point, WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies, and your conversation is handled by the resort's reservations team like any direct message.

Hosting logs

Like nearly every website, our hosting provider keeps standard technical logs (IP address, pages requested, browser type) for security and reliability. These are not used to identify or profile visitors.
