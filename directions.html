How to get here

Four easy steps to the gates.

A simple route from the city — with live WhatsApp help for the final stretch.

1

Leave Kampala city centre

Head out along Entebbe Road towards Kajjansi — roughly 30 minutes from the city centre, 20 from Entebbe Airport.

2

Watch for the Maya signage

Near Kajjansi, look out for the Maya Nature Resort signpost marking the turn off the main road.

3

Turn in at the resort entrance

Follow the access road through the greenery to the gate, where our team will welcome and direct you to parking.

4

Lost? Message us live

Send us a message and we'll share a live location pin and guide you in, turn by turn.
