Into the Green

Maya Nature Resort sits within stunning natural terrain — rolling hills, dense gardens, and thriving wildlife corridors. Our guided nature walks take you through it all, with experienced local guides who'll point out plant species, bird calls, and the resort's resident animals along the way. Whether you're an early riser for birdwatching or prefer a gentle evening stroll, there's a walk for you.

Morning & evening Expert local guides 45–90 min walks All ages welcome Binoculars available Free for guests
  • Morning walk departs at 6:30 am — best for birds and monkeys
  • Evening walk departs at 5 pm — quieter, cooler, ideal for families
  • Family-friendly pace available on request
  • Wear comfortable shoes and bring sunscreen or a light jacket
