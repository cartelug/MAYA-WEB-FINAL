Wild & Free

Uganda's Wildlife at Your Doorstep

Maya Nature Resort is home to a thriving community of wildlife — most notably the resident vervet and colobus monkeys who roam freely through the grounds. Watch them move through the treetops, forage in the gardens, and go about their daily lives just metres from you. Combined with over 40 recorded bird species, mornings at Maya can feel like the heart of a nature reserve — not a resort.
