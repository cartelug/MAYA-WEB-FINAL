The route

Three landmarks to the gate.

The simplest way in — with live help for the final stretch.

Recommended route

  1. 1

    Take the Masaka Road

    From Kampala (about 40 minutes) or Entebbe Airport (about an hour via the Expressway and Busega), head southwest on the Kampala–Masaka Road toward Kyengera and Maya.

  2. 2

    Turn off at Maya, Sun Hill

    Near Kyengera, watch for the Maya Nature Resort signage marking the turn off the main road onto Sun Hill.

  3. 3

    Arrive at the gate

    Follow the access road through the greenery to the gate, where our team welcomes you and directs you to parking.
